/ 02 — THE NIGHT

One room.
All in.

"All in is the only way out."

On May 23rd, you packed the Silk Mill and helped us launch something that matters. We worshiped together, we heard Don share how he got from the bottom back to life, and we got our first real look at what No Half Measures is built to do: walk with people through addiction, through recovery, and through everything that comes after.

None of this works from a distance. It works because people show up, look each other in the eye, and refuse to let go. That is exactly what you did on the 23rd. That is the whole mission in one room.

Below you'll find the photos from the night and Don's full talk. If it moved you, there is a way to stay in it.
